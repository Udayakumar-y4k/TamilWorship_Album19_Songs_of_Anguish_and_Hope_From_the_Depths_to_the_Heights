# We Are the Sheep of His Pasture, O Lord - கர்த்தரின் மேய்ச்சலின் ஆடுகள் நாங்கள்

Psalm 100 – Call to joyful worship: we are His people, sheep of His pasture. God made us, we belong to Him. Echoes Psalm 23 (Lord my Shepherd) and John 10:11–14 (Jesus the Good Shepherd).
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, Good Shepherd,
thank You for making us Your sheep.
Lead us in Your pasture, gather us close.
In the eternal day, let us dwell in Your fold forever.
Come quickly, our Shepherd.
