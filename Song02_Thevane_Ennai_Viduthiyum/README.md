# O God, Deliver Me - தேவனே என்னை விடுவியும்

Psalm 70 – Urgent plea for swift deliverance from enemies, shame on persecutors, joy for seekers. Echoes Psalm 40:13–17 (similar plea) and foreshadows Christ’s suffering and vindication.
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, swift Deliverer,
hasten to help us in need.
Turn back our foes in shame, let seekers rejoice in You.
In the eternal day, let us praise Your great salvation.
Come quickly, our Help and Redeemer.
