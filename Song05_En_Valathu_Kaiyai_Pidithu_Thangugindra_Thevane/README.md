# Thou God Who Holds My Right Hand - என் வலது கையைப் பிடித்துத் தாங்குகின்ற தேவனே

Psalm 73 – Asaph envies the wicked’s prosperity, questions purity’s worth, until entering sanctuary and seeing their sudden ruin. God holds his right hand, guides, receives in glory. Fulfilled in Christ (Hebrews 13:5).
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, who holds our right hand,
thank You for guiding through envy and doubt.
Lead us by Your counsel, receive us in glory.
In the eternal day, let us stand with You forever.
Come quickly, our Guide and Portion.
