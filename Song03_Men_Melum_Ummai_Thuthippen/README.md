# மேன் மேலும் உம்மைத் துதிப்பேன் - I Will Praise You More and More

Psalm 71 – Lifelong trust and praise from youth to old age. God as refuge, teacher, deliverer from depths; righteousness exalted, strength for the weak. Echoes Psalm 22:9–10 (from womb) and Isaiah 46:4 (carry to old age).
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, eternal refuge and strength,
thank You for guiding from youth to gray hairs.
Let me praise You more and more, trust You alone.
In weakness, uphold me; in the eternal day, raise me high.
Come quickly, our Sustainer.
