# உருக்கமான இரக்கத்தினால் என்னைக் கடாட்சித்தருளும் | In tender mercy, look upon me, O Lord | ஜெய் யேசு கிறிஸ்து Song 1

Psalm 69 – Cry from deep waters of reproach, shame, and gall/vinegar mockery. Plea for mercy, rescue from enemies, blotting out of wicked from book of life, hope in God’s salvation for Zion. Foreshadows Christ’s suffering (John 15:25; Romans 15:3).
Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, in tender mercy look upon us.
You know our shame and reproach; come to our soul, release us from bonds.
Hide not Your face; hear our cry in affliction.
In the eternal day, let us dwell in Your salvation and praise.
Come quickly, our Merciful Savior.
ஆமென். அல்லேலூயா!
